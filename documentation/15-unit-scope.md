# Unit Scope — MVP

This document defines the **authoritative unit roster and progression scope** for the Dice Goblins MVP. Any unit, role, tier, or promotion path not listed here is **out of scope** for MVP.

---

## 1. Design Goals

The MVP unit roster must:
- Validate tier-based progression (Tier 1 → Tier 3)
- Exercise frontline vs backline positioning
- Support core combat roles
- Minimize balance and content overhead

The focus is system validation, not roster breadth.

---

## 2. Tier Model

- Unit tiers function similarly to Pokémon evolutions
- Promotions replace the previous unit
- Promotions reset level to 1 and increase base stat floors

### Tier Support in MVP
- Tier 1: Enabled
- Tier 2: Enabled
- Tier 3: Enabled

No tiers beyond Tier 3 exist in MVP.

---

## 3. Supported Combat Roles

The MVP supports exactly four combat roles:

1. Frontline Melee (Tank / Bruiser)
2. Backline Ranged (DPS)
3. Support
4. Control

No hybrid or off-role units exist in MVP.

---

## 4. Promotion Targets

### Tier 3 Targets
- One **Frontline Melee** unit capable of reaching Tier 3
- One **Backline Ranged** unit capable of reaching Tier 3

Each Tier 3 unit requires a full Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3 promotion chain.

### Tier 2-Only Targets
- One **Support** unit capped at Tier 2
- One **Control** unit capped at Tier 2

Tier 2-only units do not have Tier 3 promotions in MVP.

---

## 5. Unit Count Breakdown

| Role | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 | Total |
|----|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| Frontline Melee | 1 | 1 | 1 | 3 |
| Backline Ranged | 1 | 1 | 1 | 3 |
| Support | 1 | 1 | — | 2 |
| Control | 1 | 1 | — | 2 |
| **Total** | | | | **10** |

The MVP unit roster contains **exactly 10 unit types**.

---

## 6. Promotion Structure

- Promotions are linear in MVP
- Branching promotion paths are planned post-MVP
- Promotion requirements:
  - Three max-level units of the same tier
  - Tier 3 additionally requires a region-specific item

Promotion costs and outcomes are deterministic.

---

## 7. Abilities Per Unit

Each unit type has:
- 2 active abilities
- Up to 2 passive traits

No unit exceeds 4 total abilities in MVP.

---

## 8. Explicit Non-Goals

The MVP unit system does **not** include:
- Branching evolution paths
- Hybrid roles
- Unit-specific dice restrictions
- Unit-specific loot tables
- Cosmetic variants tied to mechanics

---

## 9. MVP Validation Criteria

The unit system is considered MVP-complete when:
- A player can promote at least one unit to Tier 2
- A player can attempt a Tier 3 promotion
- Frontline and backline positioning materially affects outcomes
- Support and control units provide non-damage value
