Name: Dart
Type: Kobold
Role: Skirmisher
Image: 11-dart.jpg

Appearance: A lean, agile kobold with terrier-like snout, alert ears, mottled fur, obsidian-like dorsal scales, and a whip-like balancing tail.

Armor/Clothing: Lightweight feather-and-leather vest, motion-first limb wraps, and minimal gear for silent movement.

Equipment: Long staff-sling (fustibalus), stone and powder ammunition bandolier, and curved horn sidearm.

Gameplay Role Fantasy: Fast ranged skirmisher that repositions and applies opportunistic burst from range.

Silhouette and Readability Cues: Long staff-sling profile, slim runner frame, feather accents, and high-mobility posture.

Ability Telegraph Cues: Sling wind-up arc before launch shots, quick sidestep-reset motion before repeated attacks.

Threat Priority Cue: Mobility plus burst angles can punish backline exposure quickly.

- Portrait crop safe zone: snout, sling head, and feather accents should remain visible.
- Icon readability: keep sling silhouette and dorsal scale stripe readable.
- Contrast notes: preserve fur-pattern contrast against rocky and dark backdrops.
