Name: Barricade
Type: Goblin
Role: Guardian
Image: 06-barricade.jpg

Appearance: A squat, extremely broad male goblin with scarred mossy-green skin, heavy brow, and reinforced head studs.

Armor/Clothing: Massive chest guard from industrial drum or truck fender over layered burlap and tire rubber, asymmetrical pauldrons from household and automotive scrap.

Equipment: Huge reinforced door-shield with narrow viewing slit and graffiti motifs, plus short heavy rebar club with concrete weight.

Gameplay Role Fantasy: Defensive wall unit that controls lanes and blocks access to vulnerable allies.

Silhouette and Readability Cues: Very wide torso, oversized rectangular shield, low center of gravity, compact heavy weapon.

Ability Telegraph Cues: Shield brace pose before mitigation effects, short body coil before heavy counterstrike.

Threat Priority Cue: He can stall progression and absorb key damage windows if not handled.

Portrait/UI Requirements:
- Portrait crop safe zone: shield corner, face, and chest plate should remain visible.
- Icon readability: preserve shield outline and viewing slit at small sizes.
- Contrast notes: use bright edge highlights on shield rivets to avoid silhouette loss.

Art Prompt Summary:
- Broad goblin guardian built as a mobile barricade.
- Massive improvised door-shield with rebar reinforcement.
- Drum/fender chest armor and junkyard asymmetry.
- Low, immovable defensive stance.
- Rebar club with concrete striking head.
- Heavy industrial scavenger visual language.
