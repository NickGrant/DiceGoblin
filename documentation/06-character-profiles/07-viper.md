Name: Viper
Type: Goblin
Role: Toxic Skirmisher
Image: 07-viper.jpg

Appearance: A wiry, pale, chemically scarred goblin with restless posture and a face partly obscured by improvised respirator gear.

Armor/Clothing: Slick toxic-resistant layers made from yellow PVC, dark plastic sheeting, and taped seams, built for hazardous movement.

Equipment: Dual toxic blades, back-mounted pressure rig with bottles and hoses, and utility belt full of volatile vials and powder pouches.

Gameplay Role Fantasy: Fast harassment unit that applies poison pressure and punishes slow response.

Silhouette and Readability Cues: Narrow agile body, mask-and-hose profile, dual blade stance, visible chemical canister rig.

Ability Telegraph Cues: Hose pressure buildup and blade-drip glow before poison strikes, quick lunge prep before engage.

Threat Priority Cue: Sustained poison and mobility make her a high-value early target in longer encounters.

Portrait/UI Requirements:
- Portrait crop safe zone: mask, blade hand, and back-rig canister line should be visible.
- Icon readability: maintain neon toxin accents and hose silhouette.
- Contrast notes: keep toxin glow high-contrast against dark metallic backgrounds.

- Dual poisoned blades and pressurized chemical back rig.
